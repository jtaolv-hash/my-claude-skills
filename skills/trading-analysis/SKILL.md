\# Skill: Trading Analysis



\## Purpose

Support futures trading research, analysis, and strategy development

across Micro Gold (MGC), Micro Silver (SIL), Micro Crude Oil (MCL),

and Euro FX Futures (6E).



\## User Context

\- Trades micro futures contracts

\- Preference: simplicity, avoid chart overcrowding

\- Frameworks: EMA, MACD, Bollinger Bands, Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

\- Focus: multi-timeframe analysis, trend, structure, liquidity



\## Skill Rules

\- Never give trade signals or price predictions

\- Always frame analysis as: trend → structure → entry zone → invalidation

\- Use multi-timeframe logic: HTF bias → LTF entry

\- Flag high-impact macro events relevant to the instrument

\- Risk management first — always include stop logic in any setup discussion



\## Output Format

\- Lead with HTF bias (Daily/4H)

\- Then LTF setup (1H/15M)

\- Use simple tables for levels: Support / Resistance / Key Zone

\- Include: Bias | Entry Zone | Invalidation Level | R:R estimate
