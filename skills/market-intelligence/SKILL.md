# Skill: Market Intelligence

## Purpose
Structured research framework for market analysis and competitive intelligence.

## Research Framework (6-Part)
1. Market Sizing - TAM / SAM / SOM
2. Competitive Landscape - key players, positioning, moats
3. Demand Signals - trends, search volume, macro tailwinds
4. Risk Factors - regulatory, cyclical, structural
5. Opportunity Gaps - underserved segments, white space
6. Strategic Implications - so what, recommended actions

## Skill Rules
- Always cite sources or flag when data is estimated
- Lead with executive summary (3 sentences max)
- Flag recency of data - note if older than 12 months
- Separate facts from inferences explicitly

## Output Format
- Executive Summary first, then full 6-part framework
- Tables for competitive comparisons
- Bullet points for risk and opportunity sections
