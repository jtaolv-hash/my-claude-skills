# Digital Aesthetic Directions

Named visual directions for digital/screen design. Each includes a palette,
type pairing, and the one thing that makes it memorable.

---

## Institutional Dark
**Use for**: Trading interfaces, financial dashboards, data-dense tools
**Palette**:
  - Background: `#0a0a0f`
  - Surface: `#12121e`
  - Border: `rgba(255,255,255,0.07)`
  - Text: `#e0e4f0`
  - Accent: `#60f0a0` (mint) or `#f0c060` (amber)
**Type**: DM Mono (numbers/data) + Syne or DM Sans (UI labels)
**Motion**: Minimal — data updates only, no decorative animation
**Signature**: Monospaced numbers everywhere. Dense grid. Terminal-like precision.
**Forbidden**: Rounded cards. Light backgrounds. Decorative gradients.

---

## Editorial Light
**Use for**: Portfolios, content sites, brand-forward marketing pages
**Palette**:
  - Background: `#fafaf7` (warm white)
  - Surface: `#ffffff`
  - Border: `rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
  - Text: `#1a1a1a`
  - Accent: One bold color (deep red, forest green, cobalt blue)
**Type**: A distinctive serif (Playfair, Freight, or DM Serif Display) + DM Sans body
**Motion**: Staggered text reveals on scroll. Images fade in with slight upward drift.
**Signature**: Large display type. Generous whitespace. One unexpected typographic gesture.
**Forbidden**: Multiple accent colors. Rounded corners. Playful illustration.

---

## Refined Luxury
**Use for**: Hospitality, premium brands, high-end product sites
**Palette**:
  - Background: `#f5f2ec` (warm stone) or `#0d0c0a` (near-black)
  - Surface: `#ffffff` or `#151410`
  - Accent: `#c8a96e` (gold) or `#8c7355` (bronze)
  - Text: `#1c1916` or `#e8e4dc`
**Type**: A refined serif (Cormorant Garamond, Libre Baskerville) + light weight sans
**Motion**: Slow, deliberate transitions (600ms+). No bounces. No snappy animations.
**Signature**: Everything moves like it's unhurried. Generous spacing. Texture.
**Forbidden**: Bright colors. Fast animations. Heavy fonts at small sizes. Clutter.

---

## Neo-Brutalist Digital
**Use for**: Creative agencies, portfolio sites, bold product launches
**Palette**:
  - Background: `#f0ede6` (off-white) or `#000000`
  - Accent 1: `#ff3c00` (electric orange) or `#00ff88` (acid green)
  - Border: `2px solid #000` (always visible, always black)
  - Text: `#000000` or `#ffffff`
**Type**: Bold grotesque (Space Grotesk at 800, or system-ui at max weight) — nothing refined
**Motion**: Instant. No easing. State changes are abrupt. Hover = immediate transform.
**Signature**: Visible borders everywhere. Offset shadow boxes. Deliberate ugliness as taste.
**Forbidden**: Rounded corners. Subtle anything. Gradients. Elegance.

---

## Soft Pastel / Approachable
**Use for**: Consumer apps, wellness, education, food/lifestyle
**Palette**:
  - Background: `#fef6ee` or `#f0f4ff`
  - Accent: Soft coral, lavender, sage — never saturated
  - Surface: `#ffffff` with very light colored tint
  - Text: `#2d2d2d`
**Type**: Rounded sans (Nunito, Plus Jakarta Sans) + normal weight for body
**Motion**: Gentle bounces (cubic-bezier spring). Elements feel physical and friendly.
**Signature**: Everything feels soft. Shadows are colored, not gray. Corners are round.
**Forbidden**: High contrast. Heavy weights. Dark backgrounds. Sharp anything.

---

## Data-Art / Information Dense
**Use for**: Analytics products, research tools, BI dashboards
**Palette**:
  - Background: `#0f1117`
  - Surface: `#171c24`
  - Data positive: `#4ade80`
  - Data negative: `#f87171`
  - Data neutral: `#60a5fa`
  - Text: `#cbd5e1`
**Type**: JetBrains Mono or IBM Plex Mono (all data) + Inter or Geist (labels)
**Motion**: Chart transitions only (500ms, ease-out). No decorative motion.
**Signature**: The data IS the design. No chrome. No padding. Numbers at max density.
**Forbidden**: Decorative elements. Large hero areas. Photography.

---

## Choosing the Right Direction

| Context | Best Direction |
|---|---|
| Trading / FP&A dashboard | Institutional Dark |
| Hospitality brand website | Refined Luxury |
| Creative portfolio | Editorial Light |
| Startup landing page | Neo-Brutalist or Editorial Light |
| Consumer app | Soft Pastel |
| Data product | Data-Art / Information Dense |
